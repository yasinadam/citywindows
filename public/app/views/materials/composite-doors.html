Composite Doors

Composite doors are growing in popularity through the massive advantages they bring over wooden and UPVC doors. Composite doors are extra strong and thermal efficient, this means lower energy bills and home insurance premiums. Our bespoke doors are manufactured to a very high standard and spec.

Available in a wide range of colors like oak, cream, darkwood, blue, green, white, black, red etc... and styles with optional add-on accessories allowing for a greater customization to fit your individual home look.
