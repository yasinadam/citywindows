Timber Windows

Timber windows are amazing to look at and perform great against the weather. With double or triple glazing options and a wide array of color choices timber windows can really finish the look of a home. Timber windows are easy to maintain and present a natural beautiful look.

We can manufacture a wide range of styles from sash to cottage or georgian which are all made to measure, any unique size or shapes we can produce.Our timber windows have excellent thermal performance and meet the current building regulations.
