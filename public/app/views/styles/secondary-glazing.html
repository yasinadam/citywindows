Secondary Glazing

Secondary glazing is adding another slim-line window in front of your existing windows. It is an effective and cheap way of insulating and sound proofing your home. It is an ideal solution if you are unable to replace your current window setup with double glazing.

Secondary glazing is a winning solution for listed buildings and conservation areas. Adding secondary glazing is reversible.
