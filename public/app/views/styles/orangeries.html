Orangeries

Orangeries provide a different viewpoint on the classic conservatories. An orangery is much more of a constructed room with brick pillars and is a great way to add value, light and space to your home.

Presently orangeries are really popular and are most often the preferred option for new double glazed extensions, we find they are most likely to be positioned as kitchens or dining areas.
